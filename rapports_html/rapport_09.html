Requête n°9


Vitesse moyenne d'un train

SQL> SELECT train_id || ' ' || s1.city || ' - ' || s2.city "Numéro de train et Trajet",
2 ROUND(distance/((arrival_time - departure_time)*24),0) || ' km/h' AS "Vitesse Moyenne du train"
3 FROM t_train t
4 JOIN t_station s1
5 ON t.departure_station_id = s1.station_id
6 JOIN t_station s2
7 ON t.arrival_station_id = s2.station_id
8 ORDER BY train_id;
